Guru Gita — गुरुगीता
ॐ
Sri Guru Gita
Skanda Purana · Uttara Khanda · 352 Shlokas · 3 Adhyayas
The Guru Gita is a sacred dialogue from the Skanda Purana, spoken by Lord Shiva to Goddess Parvati on Mount Kailasa. When Parvati saw Shiva — worshipped by all the gods — bowing in deep reverence, she asked: "To whom do you bow?" From that question arose this profound scripture on the glory of the Guru. Every syllable is a Mantra-Raja. Its 352 shlokas span three chapters and are considered the essence of all Vedas, Shastras and Puranas.
